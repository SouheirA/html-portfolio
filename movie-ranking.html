My top 3 movies of all-time.

Manifest

Manifest is a great series where a whole plan disappears and comes back again 7 years later.
The passengers of the plan start to get visions which makes them stand out alot.

Stranger Things

Stranger things is about kids beating the way of a second world. One of the kids is a runaway from a lab and she has the ability to control stuff with her mind.

Money Heist

Money Heist has a very smart plot and its protaginst is hotly smart too. It's not the usual rob a bank or a jewelry heist. It's about robbing the money making place in Spain.
